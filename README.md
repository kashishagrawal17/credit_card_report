**Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing credit card customer demographics and transaction behavior using Power BI. Two interactive dashboards were developed to provide insights into customer segmentation and spending patterns, enabling better business decision-making.

**Objectives**     
- Analyze customer demographics (age, income, gender)
- Understand transaction trends and revenue patterns
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Track weekly revenue performance and growth

**Datasets**
1. Customer Data: Includes customer demographics such as age, gender, income, and occupation
2. Transaction Data: Contains transaction details including revenue, card category, and weekly data

**Dashboard Features**

**1. Customer Dashboard**

- Total Customers: 10.29K
- Avg Income: 57.09K
- Customer Satisfaction Score: 3.19
- Gender Distribution: Female (58.17%), Male (41.83%)
- Customer segmentation by Age Group, Income Group, and Job

**Transaction Dashboard**

- Total Revenue: 46M
- Current Week Revenue: 51K
- Previous Week Revenue: 47K
- Revenue Growth: 10.15%
- Transaction Count: 10K
- Revenue analysis by Card Category and Customer Segments

**Key Insights**
- Female customers contribute higher revenue (1.75M) compared to male customers (1.25M)
- Medium income group (3.8K customers) forms the largest segment
- Self-employed customers generate the highest revenue (0.76M)
- Blue card category dominates revenue (2.73M ~ 90% share)
- Weekly revenue shows a positive growth trend of 10.15%

**Challenges Faced**

- Data cleaning and handling data type inconsistencies
- Building correct relationships between datasets
- Writing DAX measures for weekly analysis
- Designing an interactive and user-friendly dashboard
